\subsection*{Metrics}
$n = n_+ + n_-$, $n_+ = TP + FN$, $n_- = TN + FP$\\
Accuracy: $\frac{TP+TN}{n}$, 
Precision: $\frac{TP}{TP+FP}$\\ 
Recall/TPR: $\frac{TP}{n_+}$, 
FPR: $ \frac{FP}{n_-}$\\
F1 score: $\frac{2TP}{2TP+FP+FN} = \frac{2}{\frac{1}{prec} + \frac{1}{rec}}$\\
ROC Curve: $y = $TPR, $x = $FPR
