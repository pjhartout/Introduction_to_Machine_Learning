\section{Linear Regression}
Objective, approximate:
\begin{align*}
    f(x) &= w_1x_1+\ldots+w_dx_d+w_0\\
        &= \sum_{i=1}^{d}w_ix_i+w_0\\
        &= \mathbf{w}^{T}\mathbf{x}+w_0
\end{align*}
$\forall \mathbf{x}, \mathbf{w}\in\mathbb{R}^{d}$
